Royal Poinciana at Boca Raton is a gorgeous new construction luxury gated community with an incredible East Boca Raton location. The community was built in 2007 and offers features including:

  • Full-size two-car garages
  • Resort style pool and sundeck
  • Hurricane impact resistant glass windows and doors
  • Spanish Colonial inspired architecture
  • Paved brick walkways, driveways, and sidewalks
  • "Old World" tile roofs
  • Monterrey styled balconies

There are five unique floor plans available, with 3 or 4 bedrooms and sizes ranging from 1,900 square feet to over 2,400 square feet. The spacious interiors offer high-end finishes including marble flooring, plush carpeting, master bathrooms with spa tubs, and kitchens with stainless steel appliances and granite countertops.
